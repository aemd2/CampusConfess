# CampusConfess - Moderator Guidelines
**For Student Moderators**

---

## Your Role

### What You Do
- Review reported content
- Determine if content violates guidelines
- Take appropriate action
- Document decisions

### What You DON'T Do
- See who posted (it's anonymous to you too)
- Remove posts you personally dislike
- Share moderation discussions
- Use your role for personal disputes

---

## Priority Queue

### High (Immediate)
1. Threats of violence
2. Self-harm / suicide
3. Doxxing (personal info)
4. Explicit content

### Medium (30 min)
5. Harassment
6. Hate speech
7. Academic dishonesty

### Low (2 hours)
8. Spam
9. Misinformation
10. Gray area content

---

## Actions

| Button | Action | When |
|--------|--------|------|
| Approve | Dismiss report | No violation |
| Delete | Remove post | Clear violation |
| Edit | Redact info | Good post, minor fix |
| Escalate | Send to admin | Serious/unsure |

---

## Content Decisions

### Violence - DELETE
- "I'm going to hurt [person]"
- Specific threats with targets

### Self-Harm - APPROVE + ESCALATE
- "I'm planning to end it"
- Show crisis resources, notify counseling

### Doxxing - DELETE + REDACT
- Full names, addresses, phone numbers
- Remove personal info, keep post

### Harassment - DELETE
- "That girl is such a..."
- Personal attacks on identifiable people
