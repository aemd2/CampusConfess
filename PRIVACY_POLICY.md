# CampusConfess - Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** December 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025

---

## Introduction

CampusConfess ("we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile application that allows college students to share anonymous confessions within their campus community.

**Our Core Privacy Promise:**
- Your confessions are truly anonymous
- We never store your plain-text email address
- We don't track your identity across posts
- We only collect data necessary for app functionality

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

#### Campus Verification (Required)
- **Email address (hashed only)**: When you verify with your .edu email, we immediately convert it to a SHA-256 hash. We NEVER store your actual email address.
- **Campus affiliation**: Which university you attend

#### Content You Create
- **Posts (confessions)**: Text content up to 1000 characters
- **Comments**: Replies to posts
- **Reactions**: Emoji reactions
- **Images**: Optional photos (EXIF data automatically stripped)
- **Location tags**: Optional tags like "Library" (user-selected, NOT GPS)

**Important**: Your posts, comments, and reactions are anonymous.

### 1.2 What We Do NOT Collect

- Your real name
- Your plain-text email
- GPS location data
- Contact list or phone book
- Other apps on your device
- Your browsing history

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Confirm you're a student at your claimed university
- Scan posts for threats, harassment, illegal content
- Display anonymous confessions from your campus
- Process premium feature payments
- Fix bugs and improve the app

---

## 3. Data Security

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Row Level Security**: Database-level policies prevent unauthorized access
- **Access control**: Only essential team members can access production database

---

## 4. Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of all data we have about you
- **Delete**: Delete your account and associated data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics tracking

**To Exercise Your Rights**: Email privacy@campusconfess.com
